Historical background

Ilboru Secondary School is the government school which was started in 1946 as the lutheran secondary school under the lutheran church, in 1969 Ilboru secondary schoool was taken by the government of United Republic of Tanzania and by 1972 it was a special school for high performing and talented students. Ilboru secondary school is found in the north-eastern part of Tanzania near famous tribe in East Africa the Maasai society at Arumeru district in Arusha city, Tanzania. Ilboru Secondary school is three kilometers far from the centre of the Arusha city




Ilboru secondary school is very wild, it has high number of teachers in all subjects including Science, Arts. Social and Sports subjects ,there are around 100 staff members with 80 teachers, 11 watchmen, 6 chefs and 3 student gurdians. Ilboru secondary school has about nine hundred students in both levels from form one to form six in all subjects with about six hunderd in ordinary level and three hundred in advanced level(high level)




Education and Admission

Education and admisssion at ilboru secondary school explains how the students get admitted to the school and their academics studies according to the level of their education depending the level of the education student is in the school.

Education

Ilboru secondary school offers both levels at the secondary stage the Ordinary level education and the Advanced level education as the Tanzania education system requires. The Ordinary level education(o-level) ends at form four in which the students sits for the last examination(national examination) also the advanced level education(high level) which ends at form six with the national examination. Also Ilboru secondary offers room for the private students to sit for their final year examination , so this is how wild Ilboru secondary school is.

Admission

Admission process is through the selection which is done by the national Examination cauncil. Admission at Ilboru secondary school is little bit hard and competitive, since the type of student that are selected are those with high marks in their core subjects , So the selected students in both levels of education become the part of community of the school as soon as the classes start in beginning of the academic year.

Carriculum

Ilboru secondary school uses the Tanzania education Carriculum which was introduced by national examination council(NACTE) some years ago. This carriculum is followed by all other schools in Tanzania which are under NACTE. Ilboru secondary school is registerded under NACTE with registration number S0110. provides both Ordinary and Advanced level education using the Tanzanian syllabus the school has many interactive programs and alot of student's learning facilities such as school library, classes, and well equiped laboratory which enhance the practical teaching and also the computer laboratory . The students are selected by the government according to their result in grade seven national examination.

Vision and Mission

Ilboru secondary schools carries a glorious purpose to the Ilboru community to the outside community till to the national level. The vision of the Ilboru secondary school is to build the innovative society through the high talented students who are gifted with the ability to think and to come up with new idea which will help them and the nation to develop and support the quality of citizens of Tanzania.

Our Mission

The mission is to ensure that the school support the students' dreams that will help to build strong , developed and well educated societies in the coming days.
